About science communication

If I had a dream job, it would be in science communication, specifically in Singapore, collaborating with the government, universities, and people, &c, to discuss scientific concepts and the latest developments, not only in traditional STEM fields, but also in the humanities, arts, and social sciences. It would be a dream job for me because of several reasons. It would bring financial stability into my life. It would allow me to do something I am passionate about, i.e. discussing exciting, relevant and important concepts and applications. A part of me realises just how far-reaching science is in our daily lives, and how science communication fits into that picture. Science communication is entering into a new era, and I wonder how I might play a part in all of this. The short answer is it would be a great 9-5.

About content creation

I create explainer videos on YouTube and write essays on my blog. Through this, I hope to cultivate my communication skills while exploring new concepts and fields. My current area of interest is in the philosophy of science, and the philosophy of Singapore.

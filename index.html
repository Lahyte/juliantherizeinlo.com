About science communication

A career in science communication has been a long-standing goal of mine, because despite my years of amateur content creation, my passion for it remains. I am inspired by contemporary science communicators who popularise mathematics, physics, and philosophy on YouTube and Tiktok. However, the humanities, arts, and social sciences are currently under-represented in the field. Singapore is under-represented. Thus, I ask myself, how might I contribute within this field?

About content creation

I make explainer videos on YouTube and essays on my blog. Through this, I hope to cultivate my communication skills while exploring new concepts and fields. My current interests are in philosophy of science, and Singapore's culture.
